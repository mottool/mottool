ABOUT

Run by one person, with help from the people who walk in.

mottool was started in 2026 in Dunsan, Daejeon. The shop occupies a small second-floor room above a stationery store. We carry around 800 titles at any time, run a slow publishing imprint, and host a monthly zine workshop.

We work with small presses in Korea, Japan, and Europe. We send books carefully. We try not to be in a hurry.
